Information we collect and how its used

Usually, your username cannot be traced back to you except if it contains your name or if you also use it on other platforms. Because of that, we protect your username the same way we protect personal data.


Your password is already concealed on your computer (hash) and will then be stored with more security (hash + salt) on our servers. So your password will never reach our servers in plaintext and is extra secured.


We also save the point in time of your registration to create statistics. Besides that, we have to save that time to be able to prove that you have accepted this policy.


All settings of your Minecraft server and all files that are created by your Minecraft server are saved on our servers. Furthermore, we may send this data to remote backup servers


To be able to understand what happened when errors or problems occur we save miscellaneous activities on our website in a log file, e. g. when you start your server, who enters or leaves your server, when you up- or download a world or when you change your email address or password.

Support (so we can help you with issues that were caused by changes you made)

Access Logs

We collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our Service, the Access Logs.


This access logs include information such as your IP address, browser type, browser version, the pages of our service that you visit, the time and date of your visit, we may also trace your IP to the country that it is registered to for security and intrusion prevention/detection reasons, as permited by GDPR article 6, paragraph 1, point F.


Cookies

Cookies are files with small amount of data, which may include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a web site and stored on your computer's hard drive.


We use "cookies" to collect information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you wont be able to use our service.


Tracking and Analytics Tools

We use Google Analytics which help us know how users use our website so we can optimize and work on the most used features.


Email Messages

We may email you with updates or news from the website, we may also send you emails including but not limited to your account, such login alerts, security notifications, administrative updates to content you posted


We will never spam you with non-website related emails and will never provide your email to third parties


If you do not desire to receive emails from us please contact us in order to have your account deleted


Security

The security of your Personal Information is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.


Legal

If required by law, will you disclose personal information of users to law enforcement agents.


Changes To This Privacy Policy

Right to be forgotten
